ABOUT US



At Sandy_Art_Gallery, we believe in the power of art to inspire, transform, and bring joy to everyday life. Our shop is a celebration of creativity, offering a curated collection of unique, handcrafted pieces that reflect the diverse talents of our community of artists.

Founded in 2020, Sandy_Art_Gallery started as a small passion project. Our founder, SANDHIYA envisioned a space where art enthusiasts could discover and support emerging artists while finding beautiful, one-of-a-kind pieces for their homes and lives. What began as a modest endeavor has grown into a vibrant hub for artistic expression, connecting artists and art lovers from all over the world.

Located in the vibrant heart of [Manavalanagr, Thiruvallur], we are dedicated to providing artists of all levels with the tools, inspiration, and community they need to bring their creative visions to life. Since the last 4 years, We have 250+ Customes Trusted our service to keep touch with us.



- OUR MISSION -



Our mission is simple: to bring you art that speaks to your soul. We are committed to showcasing a wide range of styles and mediums, ensuring that there is something for everyone. Whether you're looking for a striking painting to complete your living room, a delicate piece of jewelry to make a statement, or a unique gift for a loved one, you'll find it here.
